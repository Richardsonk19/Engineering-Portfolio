Personal Daily Driver FPV Drone

High performance 5" quad I fly regularly for fun and FPV video work.

Project Overview:

• Carbon Frame: Built on the SpeedyBee Master 5 V2 carbon frame for max performance and durability for real-world flying.

• Optimized Setup: Applied learnings from my previous drone builds to choose components that match my needs: power, reliability, and stable/clean video.

• Real-World Use: I fly this quad regularly for freestyle practice and client FPV video work.

Custom programmed launch takeoff

Real-World flight setup and tuning:

• Safety Setup: Configured GPS Rescue, failsafe behavior, and return settings to make crashes and signal loss less risky.

• Rates + PID Feel: Dialed in rates and PID behavior through test flights to get a locked-in feel that matches my flying style.

• Control Link Profiles: Built radio link profiles for different flight types: long-range stability vs fast, responsive freestyle, by adjusting RX settings like packet rate, power, and frequency.

• Telemetry Configuration: Configured telemetry data connection to my radio for live battery/signal information and live GPS coordinates for easier recovery after a crash.

SPLIT DESIGN ENABLES CLEAN TPU PRINTING ORIENTATION

SEAM-WELDED ASSEMBLY CREATES A STRONG FINAL MOUNT

CUSTOM 3D-PRINTED PARTS:

• Electronics Mounts: Designed and iterated TPU mounts to cleanly package and protect key components while keeping the build easy to service.

• GPS Placement: Positioned the GPS away from carbon and high-noise electronics to improve satellite lock and reliability.

• ELRS Antenna Layout: Mounted diversity receiver antennas in different orientations (horizontal + vertical) for more consistent link performance and penetration.

• O3 Antenna Position: Elevated the O3/VTX antenna above the stack for better signal clearance, range, and durability.

• Insta360 Mount: Built a horizontal camera mount to improve weight balance and keep the quad feeling more stable in flight.

• Open Source: Published these models for free on my MakerWorld to contribute back to the FPV community.
